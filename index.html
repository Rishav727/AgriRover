How Our system Works

A closed-loop system for automated crop health analysis and treatment through precise pesticide sprinkling.

1. Data Capture

The AgriRover navigates fields autonomously, using its 32 high-resolution cameras to capture detailed footage of every plant from multiple angles.

2. ML Analysis

Footage is instantly sent to our cloud API where a proprietary Machine Learning model analyzes images for signs of disease, pests, and nutrient deficiencies.

3. Diagnosis & Solution

The AI determines the precise infection level and recommends a targeted solution, identifying the exact type and amount of pesticide needed.

4. Action & Reporting

Key images are stored in a database for your records. If treatment is needed, a signal is sent back to the rover to sprinkle the precise dosage on the affected plant before moving on.

Field Case Studies

Late Blight

Fungal infection causing circular lesions with a "bulls-eye" pattern. If left untreated, it leads to significant yield loss.

Solution: Targeted application of 0.5ml Chlorothalonil fungicide directly on affected foliage.

Corn Leaf Aphids

Small insects found in dense colonies, sucking sap from the plant, which can hinder growth and vector viruses.

Solution: Precision sprinkle of insecticidal soap. Rover marked area for follow-up inspection.

Powdery Mildew

A common fungal disease that appears as white powdery spots on leaves and stems, inhibiting photosynthesis.

Solution: Rover administered a potassium bicarbonate solution, an effective organic fungicide.

Nitrogen Deficiency

Identified by uniform yellowing of older, lower leaves. Stunts plant growth and drastically reduces crop yield.

Solution: Rover flagged the 5x5m zone. Recommended a follow-up with nitrogen-rich fertilizer.
